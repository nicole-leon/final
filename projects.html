Figments is a murdery mystery novella, the first story of an upcoming duology. With themes surrounding the supernatural, science fiction, and horror, the book is featured as a trick of the imagination, which should be scrambling to piece together all the individual perspectives of the main characters into a cohesive story before their lives are lost to an impending darkness. Follow Fabian, Alzain, and Maverick as their lives twist into an haunting reality.

The sequel to Figments, following the events that take place directly after the finish of the first novel. With our main characters at a desperate loss of how to conduct their lives moving forward with the missing third, WORKING TITLE finalizes this thrilling duology with more strife, chaos, and mystery than presented before. With only a few of their questions answered and a lot more questions, can our main characters finally tie a bow on their progress work or was it too late?
