Introducing Nicole León...

...an eighteen year old student with vivid passions and hopeful expectations for her future. Her goals include user experience design and research, traveling, and writing her first novel. Nicole has a multiple projects ongoing she hopes to showcase one day.

With Nicole's sights set on entrepreneurship and goals on creating a business surrounding immersive stories and intricate worlds, Nicole looks to bring her stories to life with interactive design via human feedback. She will be attending Penn State University in the fall of 2022 to study Human Centered Design and Development. With her love for writing combined with her studies in information science and technology, there are no limits on Nicole's capabilities.
