Nicole León is a graduating from Strongsville High School in the spring of 2022 to pursue Human Centered Design and Development at Penn State University. She will be graduating with a 4.5 weighted GPA and is involved with numerous school organizations. A member of National Honor Society, section leader of the alto saxophones in marching band and wind ensemble, Nicole's talents stretch between academics and music for her school life.

Outside, Nicole volunteers at a local hospital's weight management program as well pursuing creative avenues, such as writing, on the side. In addition to her future in a user experience career, Nicole hopes to publish several novels while personally promoting and marketing the material she's crafted. With a very wide world view from a mixed background, Nicole looks forward to traveling the world and learning many languages. Interested in other cultures besides that of her own and her peers, Nicole's constant strive to become more fufilled incorporates each of these cultivated pieces of her life.

Nicole would like to thank her family and friends for standing alongside her during the lovely and not so lovely times. The thanks extends to Janeen and Carlos, to Corina, to Kayla, Caroline, Megan, Zoe, and Ella, to Henry and Brendan, to Kristin and Riya, Alex and Ozzy, and many more.
